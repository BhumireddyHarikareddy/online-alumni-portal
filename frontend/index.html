About MCA & JNTUA CEA

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program at JNTUA College of Engineering, Anantapur (CEA) has been nurturing talented graduates for years. Our alumni have excelled in IT, software development, research, and entrepreneurship across India and abroad. This portal is dedicated to connecting MCA alumni, sharing achievements, career opportunities, and organizing events to keep the MCA spirit alive.

JNTUA CEA, established as a premier engineering institution, continues to focus on innovation, practical learning, and research excellence. The MCA department emphasizes both technical expertise and holistic development, preparing students for the dynamic IT industry.
